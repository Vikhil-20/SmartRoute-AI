# Software Requirements Specification (SRS)

# SmartRoute AI

## 1. Introduction

SmartRoute AI is a full-stack logistics optimization platform that intelligently plans delivery routes using graph algorithms and real-time decision making.

The system helps delivery companies minimize delivery time, fuel consumption, and operational costs while maximizing customer satisfaction.

---

## 2. Purpose

The purpose of SmartRoute AI is to provide an intelligent delivery management system capable of:

- Managing customers
- Managing drivers
- Managing warehouses
- Optimizing delivery routes
- Tracking deliveries
- Providing analytics

---

## 3. Scope

The system will support:

- Customer registration
- Driver registration
- Admin dashboard
- Warehouse management
- Delivery management
- Route optimization
- Live tracking
- Analytics dashboard

---

## 4. Stakeholders
